About

Ogbomoso
Ignite

Ogbomoso Ignite is the premier technology and entrepreneurship event that brings together innovators, creators, and visionaries from across Nigeria and beyond. Our mission is to spark the "ignite" moment where brilliant ideas meet the resources and connections needed to transform them into reality.

Join us as we explore the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship, featuring inspiring speakers, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities designed to fuel your entrepreneurial journey. This is where the future of innovation begins.

Speakers

Ogundimu

FOUNDER & CEO, TECHAFRICA

A visionary entrepreneur and technology leader with over 15 years of experience building innovative solutions across Africa. Adebayo has founded three successful startups and is passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems.

Adesina

DATA SCIENTIST, FINTECH NIGERIA

Funmi
Adebayo

UX DESIGN LEAD
DIGITAL LAGOS

An award-winning UX designer passionate about creating inclusive digital experiences for African users. Funmi leads design innovation at one of Nigeria's largest government digital transformation initiatives.
