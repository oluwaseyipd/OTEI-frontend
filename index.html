About

What is Ogbomoso
Tech & Entrepreneurship Ignite?

Ogbomoso Tech & Entrepreneurship Ignite is a flagship innovation event designed to spark technology adoption, entrepreneurial thinking, and regulatory compliance among students, professionals, and small businesses. We bridge the gap between brilliant ideas and successful execution by addressing critical challenges in technology access, business structure, tax education, and regulatory compliance.

Through keynote sessions, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities, we create an ecosystem where industry leaders, tech experts, policymakers, and investors converge. Our focus spans technology & digital innovation, business growth, startup funding, tax education, and youth empowerment—building a sustainable innovation culture in Ogbomoso and beyond.
